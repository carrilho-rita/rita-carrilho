About Me.

I am Rita and I'm a Web Developer based in Perpignan, France. I speak Portuguese, English and French.

In the midst of these unique times and driven by curiosity, I discovered web development. After some years working in Cinema I decided to shift my goals. Currently, I'm a FullStack student at L'IDEM, a french school.

Attention to detail, problem solving & communication are some of my strenghts. I truly believe that good teamwork and challenges are the best way to grow.

Side Project.

Besides programming, I'm also passionate by Cinema.

Over the years, I developed projects of the most varied genres for tv and cinema, namely fiction, documentary, experimental and series. I've collaborated with Portuguese, English and German production companies.

After my bachelor in Cinema, I've been developing projects with two friends. We named our collective Ay Filmes.

In 2017, I was nominated for Best Cinematography award with the short film Fôlego in High Peak Independent Film Festival.
